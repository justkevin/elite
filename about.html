Elite Office Supplies deals with sourcing and supply in contract and general product/ goods supply for Government and the private sector. Elite Office Supplies was established to help ease the frustrations of sourcing and procurement of hard to locate products for various sectors of business and industry in Kenya. By utilizing a network of ISO-certified factories in Kenya and abroad, Elite Office Supplies offers a comprehensive outsourcing program that includes supplier identification and qualification, quality assurance planning and inspections, product delivery according to client specifications. .

We source and supply a wide range of items ranging from Office Furniture, IT and Office Supplies,and fittings.

As well as providing an urgent quality requirement service throughout Nairobi and major cities, we go a step further and cover Kenya as a whole. With our network of contacts we are able to locate & supply any product quickly, including locally and nationally stocked products. The company covers the entire Kenya and it is not limited by geographical boundaries. Elite Office Supplies makes no charge for submitting an enquiry for a quotation and there is no obligation to proceed once a quote is sent.

Our principal role is to ensure our clients are free to concentrate on their core activities, whilst safe in the knowledge of their working environments being secured and protected. Our expertise lies in the ability to design and animate Office solutions for any type of situation.
